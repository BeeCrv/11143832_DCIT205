About Us

The Computer Science Departmentof UG

Our computer science department has been providing top-tier computing education and research for over 4 decades. We have 5 expert faculty covering all areas of computer science from AI to security to software engineering. We offer undergraduate and graduate degree programs designed to give students the optimal blend of theoretical and practical knowledge. This allows our graduates to thrive in computing careers at leading tech companies and research institutions. With excellent faculty, bright young students, innovative corporate ties and state-of-the-art facilities, we foster an environment of creativity and exploration around computing and technology. We welcome new students, faculty, partners and collaborators excited about the future of our field.
